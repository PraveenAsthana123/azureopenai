# Enterprise Copilot - User Guide

## Getting Started

### What is Enterprise Copilot?

Enterprise Copilot is an AI-powered assistant that helps you find information across company documents. It searches HR policies, technical documentation, process guides, and other internal knowledge bases to provide accurate, cited answers.

### How to Access

**Microsoft Teams:**
1. Open Microsoft Teams
2. Search for "Enterprise Copilot" in the chat search
3. Start a conversation with the bot

**Web Portal:**
1. Navigate to https://copilot.company.com
2. Sign in with your company credentials
3. Start asking questions

---

## Asking Questions

### Best Practices for Questions

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Be specific: "What is the PTO policy for US employees?" | Be vague: "Tell me about time off" |
| Ask one question at a time | Ask multiple unrelated questions |
| Provide context: "For the Q4 sales report..." | Assume the bot knows your context |
| Use natural language | Use keywords only |

### Example Questions

**HR & Benefits:**
- "How many vacation days do I get per year?"
- "What is the process for requesting parental leave?"
- "What are the health insurance options for my family?"

**IT & Technical:**
- "How do I set up VPN on my laptop?"
- "What is the process for requesting software access?"
- "How do I reset my password?"

**Policies & Procedures:**
- "What is the expense reimbursement limit for meals?"
- "What are the guidelines for remote work?"
- "How do I report a security incident?"

**Company Information:**
- "What are the core values of the company?"
- "Who is the head of the Engineering department?"
- "Where are our office locations?"

## Understanding Responses

### Citations

Every response includes citations that reference the source documents:

```
Example Response:

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) annually [Source 1].
Unused PTO can be carried over up to 5 days [Source 2].

Sources:
[Source 1] Employee Handbook > Benefits > Time Off Policy
[Source 2] HR FAQ > PTO Carryover Guidelines
```

**Always verify important information** by clicking on the source links.

### Confidence Levels

The copilot may indicate its confidence level:

| Response Type | Meaning |
|---------------|---------|
| Direct answer with citations | High confidence - information found in documents |
| "Based on the available information..." | Medium confidence - partial match found |
| "I don't have information about..." | No matching information in knowledge base |
| "You may want to contact..." | Topic requires human assistance |

### When the Copilot Says "I Don't Know"

If you see "I don't have information about that topic," it means:
- The topic may not be in the knowledge base yet
- The question may be outside the scope of available documents
- The answer may require up-to-date information not yet indexed

**What to do:**
1. Try rephrasing your question
2. Be more specific about what you're looking for
3. Contact the relevant department directly (HR, IT, etc.)

## Follow-up Questions

### Continuing a Conversation

The copilot remembers the context of your conversation:

```
You: What is our vacation policy?
Bot: Employees receive 20 days of PTO annually...

You: Can I carry over unused days?
Bot: Yes, you can carry over up to 5 days to the next year...

You: Is there a deadline for that?
Bot: Carryover days must be used by March 31...
```

### Starting Fresh

If you want to ask about a completely new topic, simply ask your new question. The copilot will understand you've changed topics.

## Topics Covered

The Enterprise Copilot has knowledge about:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| HR & Benefits | PTO, benefits, payroll, onboarding |
| IT Support | Software access, VPN, passwords, equipment |
| Company Policies | Travel, expenses, remote work, security |
| Procedures | Approvals, requests, reporting |
| Organization | Departments, contacts, structure |

### Topics NOT Covered

The copilot cannot help with:
- Personal performance reviews or salary information
- Confidential business strategies
- Legal or medical advice
- Real-time data (stock prices, schedules)
- External information (news, competitors)

## Privacy & Security

### What We Collect

- Your questions (anonymized for improvement)
- Response quality feedback (thumbs up/down)

### What We DON'T Do

- Share your questions with other employees
- Store personal identifiers with questions
- Use your data for non-work purposes
- Provide access to documents you don't have permission to see

### Role-Based Access

The copilot respects your access permissions:
- You only see information from documents you're authorized to access
- HR-specific documents are only visible to HR team members
- Confidential documents require appropriate access groups

## FAQ

### Q: Is the copilot always right?
**A:** The copilot provides answers based on company documents, but information may be outdated. Always verify critical information with official sources or relevant departments.

### Q: Can others see my questions?
**A:** No. Your questions are private. We only use anonymized data to improve the service.

### Q: Why can't I find certain information?
**A:** The document may not be in the system yet, or you may not have access permissions. Contact the document owner or relevant department.

### Q: Can I ask about personal employee information?
**A:** No. The copilot doesn't have access to individual employee records. Contact HR for personal information.

### Q: Is this replacing the HR/IT helpdesk?
**A:** No. The copilot is a first line of support for common questions. Complex issues should still go to the appropriate team.

### Q: How often is the knowledge base updated?
**A:** Documents are synced daily. New or updated documents appear within 24 hours.

### Q: Can I use this for work with external parties?
**A:** The copilot is for internal use only. Don't share responses externally without verification.
